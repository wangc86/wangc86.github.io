Welcome!

Welcome to Networked Cyber-Physical Systems Laboratory (NCL). We study networked CPS systems with a focus on real-time performance, dependability, and efficiency. Target scenarios include performant Internet of Things (e.g., Industrial IoT) and Smart Cities.

歡迎來到網宇實體系統研究室 (NCL)。網宇實體系統、又名虛實整合系統、探討的是電腦世界與自然世界的重要交互作用。本研究室從實務應用出發，分析及建構能滿足即時性、可靠性、及高效率這三個面向的電腦系統。應用場景包含高效能物聯網 (如工業物聯網) 以及智慧城市。

Research Projects

Fault-Tolerant Real-Time Messaging for Edge Computing (2017-2019)

Meeting applications’ message-loss and latency requirements.

Cyber-Physical Event Processing Middleware (CPEP) (2015-2018)

Enforcing both application-specific latency requirement and event’s temporal validity.

Safety Software Systems Design for Self-Driving Cars (2014-2015)

A collaborative research effort with General Motors, Inc. and University of Pennsylvania, USA.

Networked Virtual Worlds (2012-2013)

A scalable networking architecture to allow effective virtual interactions.

Scheduling Wireless Sensor Networks (2009-2010)

Leveraging graph theory for low-latency and adaptive wireless transmissions.
