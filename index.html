研究室簡介 PDF

網宇實體系統在情理內有效整合物理與資訊工程，為社會服務，並一窺自然的奧妙。
Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) highlight a sensible integration of natural and computing, for the serving of this generation, and for the appreciation of the nature.
